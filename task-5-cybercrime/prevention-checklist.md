## 🛡️ Prevention Checklist — Top Tips


| # | Tip |
|---|-----|
| 1 | Never send UPI to someone unverified in person |
| 2 | Verify seller identity through 2+ independent sources |
| 3 | Use UPI app's "Verify UPI ID" before sending money |
| 4 | Set a daily UPI transaction cap in your bank app |
| 5 | Never click shortened links from unknown numbers |
| 6 | Enable 2FA on all important accounts |
| 7 | Never share your OTP with anyone, ever |
| 8 | Freeze UPI accounts immediately if phone is lost |
| 9 | Report fraud at **[cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)** |
| 10 | Save helpline **1930** — call immediately if fraud is active |

---

## 🆘 Emergency Contact

> ### Cyber Crime Helpline: `1930`
> Available **24x7** across India.
> Report online: **[cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)**
> Call immediately if fraud is in progress — early reporting improves recovery chances.
